Preview — The Rambunctious River

The morning started wrong.
The pump coughed.
The ladder slipped.
The bush fought back.
Buddy Bootz landed flat on her dignity and blamed the squirrel.


Sir Drinks-A’Lot didn’t argue. He never argued in the mornings. He simply watched, took a slow sip from his flask, and waited for the chaos to decide what it wanted.


It didn’t take long.
The river shimmered.
Not the usual shimmer. Not sunlight. Not reflection.
Something else.
Something watching back.


Buddy saw it first. Of course she did. Trouble had a habit of introducing itself to her personally.


Sir stepped forward, calm as ever, like a man who had already accepted that the day would not go according to plan.


The raft creaked.
The water shifted.
And just beneath the surface…
the river opened.
